Our story

There’s a space between your next doctor’s appointment and the ER.

We’re opening so you have somewhere close to home for the in-between things: the Saturday sprains and the Thursday afternoon fevers. We’ll get you taken care of, and coordinate with the rest of your care team to make sure you get the care you deserve.

  • Saturday afternoon
    You rolled an ankle on a trail and you need an x-ray, not the ER.
  • Thursday, 4 pm
    Your six-year-old’s fever spiked at school pickup.
  • Monday morning
    A UTI that needs attention today, not next Thursday.
  • Friday, 6 pm
    Stitches after something sharp, or a cough that just won’t quit.
What we’ll offer

What you’d expect from a walk-in clinic. And nothing you wouldn’t.

  • Walk-in care

    Everyday illness and injury. No appointment needed.

  • X-rays on site

    For suspected fractures and chest concerns, without a second stop.

  • Stitches & minor procedures

    Cuts, splints, drained abscesses, and things stuck where they shouldn’t be.

  • Rapid testing

    In-clinic tests for strep, flu, COVID, UTIs, and more. Bloodwork sent to the lab.

  • EKGs

    Heart rhythm checks when something doesn’t feel right.

  • IV fluids & hydration

    For dehydration, migraines, and getting back on your feet.

  • Physicals

    Sports, school, camp, and pre-employment. Whatever the form asks for.

  • Vaccines

    Flu shots, tetanus boosters, and travel-ready immunizations.

  • Occupational medicine

    Workers’ comp care, drug and alcohol screens, and DOT physicals.

  • Coordinated care

    Notes and results sent back to your primary care provider, so nothing falls between the cracks.

Who we are
